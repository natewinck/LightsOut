Midnight Munchies

It's bedtime, but you don't want to go to bed. You want cookies. Sneak to the kitchen without getting caught! Good luck, because the lights are out!

This is an audio-only game; we followed the Marco Polo diversifier. There is no visual component to the game. Headphones recommended.

See the "screenshot" for a rough map of the house. Use the arrow keys to turn and to walk forward and backward. You can also use WASD (QWERTY) or ZQSD (AZERTY). Listen to get your bearings!
